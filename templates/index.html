Twitter Plays Chess

Twitter Plays Chess is a social experiment leveraging collective human intelligence to play chess against an AI. You can submit a vote for a move by making a tweet with the hashtag #TwitterPlaysChess. Moves must specify the square of the piece to move and the square to move to. For example, if the game has just started, tweeting 'a2a3 #TwitterPlaysChess' will vote for the pawn in square a2 to move to a3. To vote for a new game, tweet 'newgame #TwitterPlaysChess'. To get around Twitter's limitation on posting the same tweet, you can add anything after the move itself (e.g. 'a2a3 I am awesome #TwitterPlaysChess'). Moves not of the above format will not be accepted. Every minute, the move with the largest number of votes is chosen. The AI is then given 10 seconds to determine its move.
