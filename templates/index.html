Twitter Plays Chess

Twitter Plays Chess is a social experiment leveraging collective human intelligence to play chess. You can submit a vote for a move by making a tweet with the hashtag #TwitterPlaysChess. Moves must specify the square of the piece to move and the square to move to. For example, if the game has just started, tweeting 'a2a3 #TwitterPlaysChess' will vote for the pawn in square a2 to move to a3. Moves not of this format will not be accepted.
